Gassit FAQs

Gassit is an all-in-one app that simplifies your gas needs. Whether you are a gas plant owner, distributor, or consumer, Gassit provides services like inventory management, access to loans, and home delivery of gas within an hour.

You can download the Gassit app from the App Store for iOS devices or Google Play for Android devices. Simply search for "Gassit," download, and install the app on your device.

For every gas purchase you make through the Gassit app, you earn loyalty points. These points can be accumulated and redeemed for discounts on future gas purchases, providing savings and rewards for regular use.

Yes, the Gassit app allows you to check the current gas prices instantly. This feature helps you compare prices and make informed decisions without the need to call multiple gas stations.

Gassit offers a fast home delivery service, ensuring that your gas is delivered to your doorstep within an hour of placing the order through the app.

Gassit provides access to two types of loans: BOI loans for gas plant owners and quick loans for distributors. BOI loans help increase stock purchases, while quick loans provide immediate funds for gas purchases at low-interest rates.

Gassit is expanding its service areas continuously. However, the use of Gassit depends on gas plants using the Gassit software first. Without this integration, end-users and distributors cannot utilize the Gassit app on their own. To check if Gassit is available in your area, download the app and search for your desired Gas station. Not available then send us a message.

Gassit uses advanced encryption and security protocols to protect your payment information. We are committed to ensuring that your data is secure and private.

You can reach Gassit customer support by emailing [support@gassit.com](mailto:support@gassit.io) or by visiting the "Contact Us" section within the app. Our support team is available to assist you with any questions or concerns.

Yes, Gassit caters to both individual consumers and commercial clients. Gas plant owners and distributors can manage large-scale orders, inventory, and access loans through the app, making it a versatile solution for all gas-related needs.
